ANGER

ANother Great & Easy Responsive menu

View project onGitHub

What is?

ANGER is acronym for ANother Great & Easy Responsive menu. Its purpose is to make simple to build a responsive menu starting from a wellformed HTML5 page menu.

Usage

  1. Include jQuery in your page

    <script src="//ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/1.9.1/jquery.min.js"></script>

  2. Include ANGER in your page after jQuery inclusion

    <script src="anger.js"></script>

  3. Create your simple menu

    <nav id="my-menu">
<ul>
    <li><a href="#Menu1">Menu 1</a></li>
    <li><a href="#Menu2">Menu 2</a></li>
    <li><a href="#Menu3">Menu 3</a></li>
    <li><a href="#Menu4">Menu 4</a></li>
</ul>
</nav>

  4. Call ANGER

    jQuery('#my-menu').anger();

  5. Don't be Anger... do it with ANGER! :)
